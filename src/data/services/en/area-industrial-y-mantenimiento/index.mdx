We provide comprehensive industrial maintenance services, both preventive and corrective, covering facilities, machinery and steel structures. We minimise production downtime and guarantee the continuous operability of your installations.

## Our maintenance services

### Preventive maintenance

- Scheduled inspection and maintenance plans
- Condition monitoring and early fault detection
- Maintenance records and reporting

### Corrective maintenance

- Rapid response to breakdowns and failures
- Emergency repairs
- Replacement of worn or damaged components

### Structural maintenance

- Inspection and repair of steel structures
- Anti-corrosion treatments and protective coatings
- Strengthening and reinforcement works

### Industrial facilities

- Maintenance of production lines and equipment
- Electrical and mechanical installations
- Hydraulic and pneumatic systems

## Benefits

- Reduced unplanned downtime
- Extended equipment lifespan
- Optimised operational costs
- Full traceability and documentation
