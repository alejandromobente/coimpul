COIMPUL has extensive experience in the design, fabrication and installation of steel structures for all types of buildings: industrial, commercial and civil.

We work to the highest quality standards, ensuring technical precision, durability and compliance with current regulations in every project we undertake.

## Our steel structure services

### Design & engineering

- Structural design tailored to each project
- Structural calculation and sizing
- Technical drawings and documentation

### Fabrication

- In-house workshop with state-of-the-art machinery
- Quality control at every stage of the process
- Surface treatments and corrosion protection

### Assembly & installation

- On-site structure assembly
- Coordination with other trades
- Technical supervision throughout execution

### Types of structures

- Industrial buildings and warehouses
- Roof and facade structures
- Walkways, platforms and mezzanines
- Metal canopies and pergolas
- Structures for machinery and special installations
