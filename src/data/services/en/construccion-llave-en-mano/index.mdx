We manage the complete project lifecycle, from engineering and design through to the final handover of the installation. We offer comprehensive solutions tailored to each client's needs, with a single point of contact and total peace of mind throughout the entire process.

## Our turnkey service

### Project planning

- Needs analysis and feasibility study
- Project engineering and detailed design
- Permits, licences and regulatory approvals

### Execution

- Complete management of all trades and suppliers
- Construction and installation supervision
- Quality control at every stage

### Handover

- Commissioning and testing
- As-built documentation
- Post-handover support and warranty

## Why choose turnkey?

- **Single point of contact**: one team manages everything
- **Cost certainty**: agreed budget with no surprises
- **Time efficiency**: optimised scheduling and coordination
- **Quality assurance**: full responsibility from start to finish
