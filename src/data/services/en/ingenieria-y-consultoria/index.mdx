We provide specialist technical consultancy in industrial engineering: structural studies, technical inspections, site management and project consulting. We bring technical rigour and experience to optimise every phase of your industrial project.

## Our engineering services

### Structural engineering

- Structural analysis and calculation
- Load assessments and reinforcement studies
- Compliance reports and certifications

### Technical inspections

- Condition surveys of existing structures
- Safety and compliance audits
- Non-destructive testing coordination

### Site management

- Technical direction of works
- Quality control and supervision
- Coordination of trades and contractors

### Project consulting

- Feasibility studies and viability reports
- Regulatory and permitting advice
- Cost planning and budget control

## Our approach

We combine deep technical knowledge with practical on-site experience to deliver engineering solutions that are both rigorous and pragmatic. Whether you need a structural assessment, expert advice on a complex project or full site management, our team is ready to help.
