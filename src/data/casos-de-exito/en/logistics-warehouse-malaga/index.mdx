Turnkey construction of a 4,500 m² logistics warehouse at the Parque Tecnológico de Andalucía (Malaga).

## The challenge

The client required a comprehensive solution covering everything from basic engineering through to final handover, within a tight eight-month programme.

## Our solution

Coimpul took on full project management: structural design, fabrication of the steel structure in our workshop, on-site assembly and fit-out. The warehouse features a portal steel frame, sandwich panel roofing and composite panel façades.

## Results

- Delivered on time: 8 months from project start
- 380 tonnes of steel structure
- CE certification to EN-1090
- 4,500 m² of usable floor area
