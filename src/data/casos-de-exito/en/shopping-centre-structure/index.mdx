Extension of a shopping centre in Seville using a new-build steel structure fully integrated with the existing building.

## The challenge

Working within an operational building while minimising disruption to daily retail activity, with construction scheduled during night-time hours.

## Our solution

Complete off-site prefabrication of the structure to reduce on-site assembly time. Design of bespoke connections for integration with the existing concrete structure.

## Results

- 220 tonnes of steel structure
- Assembly completed in 6 weeks
- Zero safety incidents
- Full integration with the existing building
